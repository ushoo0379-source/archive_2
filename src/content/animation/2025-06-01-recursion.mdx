Recursion is a 10-minute sci-fi animatic featuring 2D character animation. The story follows a human and a robot on an epic journey that explores the birth of self-awareness. This project was Shortlisted for the 25th Animation Academy Awards of Beijing Film Academy.

I was responsible for character design and animation. Additionally, I provided 3D-to-2D background assistance and composed original background music for the project.
